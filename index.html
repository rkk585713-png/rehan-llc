About REHAN LLC

Engineering the final mirror finish, at global scale

REHAN LLC is a precision manufacturer and exporter of diamond polishing and grinding tools for the natural and engineered stone industry. From our production floor to fabrication shops on five continents, every pad we make is built to hold a consistent bond, a true finish, and a longer working life.

20+ Years of Experience

Uncompromising Quality Assurance

Every batch is tested for bond hardness, concentricity and finish consistency before it ships.

Global Export Network
